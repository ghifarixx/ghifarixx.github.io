The Legend

More Than
A Brand.

Von Dutch began as the nickname of Kenny Howard — a pinstriper, painter, and motorcycle builder who defined the American Kustom Kulture movement. His iconic flying eyeball symbol lives on as one of the most recognized marks in streetwear history.

Now rooted in Indonesia, Von Dutch Originals brings that same spirit of rebellion, craft, and culture to the streets of Jakarta and beyond — through exclusive collabs, limited drops, and permanent flagship stores.

  • Authentic Kustom Kulture apparel & accessories
  • Exclusive IIMS × Von Dutch collaboration
  • Flagship stores in Jakarta & Bandung
  • Trucker hats, tees, jackets & limited merch
